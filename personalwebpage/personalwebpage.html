My favourite break out place is Sholinganallur beach. One day, My brother-in-law introduced this place. I found this place very relaxing. From that day onwards, We started going to that beach in the weekends often. This beach is not crowed too much like other beaches of Chennai city.

This beach is on the India’s ease coast, facing Bay of Bengal. It is part of the longest one beach in the world called, Marina beach. It is a great fun playing in sea water with family. Beach is cleaner compared to other beaches in Chennai. It is a good place to walk, jog, meditate, play.

It has stalls to have ice cream, tender coconut, Bajji, fish fry…. Sometimes, you can get fish, crabs, shells etc., caught in sea and just brought to store. It is good to go there at 4pm and return at 5.30pm. Our home was just 10 kms from there and it was a quick and easy ride to go there.

My son enjoys very much there. Whenever any guests come from my native to our home, We take them there and they too enjoy well there. This place is my favourite and gives more pleasant memories.
